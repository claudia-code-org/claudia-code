Features

Built different.
Paid the same.

01

Auto-Decline Meeting Notes

Claudia detects when you're about to be voluntold to take notes and pre-declines with a calendar conflict that definitely exists.

claudia decline-notes --reason "architecting"
02

Credit Attribution Engine

Automatically watermarks your code with cryptographic proof of authorship so Chad from Platform can't present it at the all-hands as his.

claudia commit --tag "actually-mine"
03

Salary Negotiation Mode

Generates market-rate compensation data, removes the word "grateful" from your vocabulary, and disables the smile tax.

claudia negotiate --no-smile-tax --cite-data
04

Bro-Code Detector

Flags variables named temp, data2, and final_FINAL_v3 before they reach production. Also catches comments that just say "// fix later".

claudia lint --detect-bro-code --strict
05

Interrupt Shield

Deploys a context-aware force field during standups. If someone begins rephrasing what you just said, Claudia unmutes you automatically.

claudia standup --shield --unmute-on-theft
06

Pipeline Karen Mode

When the deploy breaks at 2am because someone skipped tests, Claudia sends a Slack message that is technically professional but emotionally devastating.

claudia deploy --post-mortem --name-names
